Welcome to the world of the Flying Spaghetti Monster!
This app combines The Gospel of the Flying Spaghetti Monster and daily tips from FSM. Every day you will find new inspiration, Pastafarian wisdom and a dose of humor.

Features:
- The full text of the Gospel of the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
- Daily tips and wise quotes in Pastafarian style.
- Convenient settings for personalization.

Suitable for those who want to learn more about Pastafarianism, are interested in alternative religion, philosophy or just want to laugh and diversify their day with unusual philosophy. Let His Spaghetti Tentacle touch you too!
